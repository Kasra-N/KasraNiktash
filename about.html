About Me


A focused software developer with strong foundation in web development, and confident in various languages and technologies such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, as well as back-end languages and frameworks such as Java, C#/.NET core and PHP/Laravel. My education started in University of Simon Fraser with Bachelor's of Science. Shortly after, I decided to seek more education and focus on web and software development, which has led me to the Software Systems Developer (SSD) program at BCIT.

I enjoy spending extra time on online courses on Python and Linux (Debian) to become comfortable in writing scripts in Python and become more familiar with network security and penetration testing.

Besides coding, I am very passionate about health, fitness and training martials such as Kickboxing and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu.

I find great value in staying physically active and spending time in the nature in order to stay focused, healthy, work under pressure and be productive for long periods of time.

Relevant skills:

  • Languages: HTML, CSS, Javascript, C#, PHP, Java
  • Frameworks: ASP.NET, Laravel, React.
  • Databases: SQL, MySQL.
  • Tools: Windows, Linux, Git, Visual Studio, InjtelliJ, Atom, Vim.
